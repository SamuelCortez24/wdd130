Our Most Popular Trips

Family Float Adventure

The Family Float Adventure is the perfect way to introduce rafting to families and beginners. With calm, gentle waters and stunning views, this trip provides a relaxed experience that lets you soak in the beauty of the river. It's a great opportunity for families to bond, enjoy nature, and create lasting memories on the water.

Extreme Rapids Challenge

For those who crave adventure, the Extreme Rapids Challenge is the ultimate test. With fast-moving, intense rapids and expert-level runs, this trip is designed for experienced rafters who are looking to push their limits. Only for thrill-seekers aged 18 and over, this high-energy adventure promises heart-racing moments and unforgettable challenges.
